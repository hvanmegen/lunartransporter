# Controls

## Keyboard

- Rotate left: `ArrowLeft` or `A`
- Rotate right: `ArrowRight` or `D`
- Throttle: `ArrowUp` or `W`
- Retro: `ArrowDown` or `S`

## Gamepad

- Rotation: Left stick X axis
- Throttle: Right trigger (button 7)
- Retro: Left trigger (button 6)

## Notes

- Gamepad inputs use a deadzone (`0.2` by default).
- The input system selects the most recently active device.
